Why You’ll Love Working With Me

Frequently Asked Questions

To book your session, we require a deposit of €50 via Bizum or bank transfer. This amount will be deducted from the final price of the selected package.

If it rains on the day of your session, we’ll reschedule for the next available date that works for you. This way, we ensure you have the best conditions for beautiful photos.

After your session, it typically takes about 2 to 3 weeks for you to receive your final edited photos. This allows us time to carefully select and perfect each image, ensuring the highest quality results. If you need them sooner, just let us know, and we’ll do our best to accommodate!

If you cancel the session, the deposit is generally non-refundable, as it reserves your spot and covers preliminary arrangements. However, if you need to reschedule, we can apply your deposit to a new date, provided we receive notice at least 48 hours in advance.

For newborns, I highly recommend the Standard Package. This session takes place in the comfort of your home, allowing us to capture genuine, relaxed moments with your baby and family in a familiar environment. It focuses on the natural connection and love in your home, with minimal posing and props, resulting in timeless, heartfelt images that feel authentic.

Yes, I offer in-home photoshoots, especially for newborn lifestyle sessions and family portraits. Home sessions provide a cozy, familiar environment that can make everyone feel more relaxed and natural, especially babies and young children. We use the best natural light in your home and can incorporate meaningful elements from your space to add a personal touch to your photos.
